Custom GPT vs Gem

Comparing OpenAI's Custom GPTs and Google's Gemini Gems for District use.

Custom GPTs and Google Gems are essentially the same thing — AI assistants built with instructions and optional knowledge. They just exist on different platforms with a few small differences below.

Quick Comparison

Feature Custom GPT Google Gem
Web control Can toggle on/off No toggle
Knowledge source Upload static files Live Google Docs/Sheets
NotebookLM integration No NotebookLM Yes
